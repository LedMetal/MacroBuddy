Meal Planning Tips


Here's a general meal formula to follow for your 4-5 meals per day:

  1. Protein + Carbohydrate + Fruit
  2. Protein + Carbohydrate + Vegetables/Fruit
  3. Protein + Carbohydrate
  4. Protein + Vegetables
  5. (Optional) Protein + Vegetables

These are just suggested servings, of course.
Technically, you could complete your daily macros using just fast food.
However, remember the difference between good calories and bad calories.
So remember to make good choices and allow yourself to 'cheat' every now and then!


Suggested Servings Per Meal
  • Eggs: 6 egg whites
  • Low Fat Cottage Cheese: 1 cup
  • Chicken Breast: baked, grilled or pan-fried
  • Lean Ground Beef: 6 oz
  • Pork: 6 oz (lean pork chops or pork tenderloin)
  • Fish: 6 oz (any fish!)
  • Protein Powder with Water: 1 scoop (~25g of protein)
  • Deli Meat: 4 oz turkey (low sodium)
  • Oatmeal: 3/4 cup
  • High Fiber Cereal: 1 cup with 1/2 cup skim milk
  • Sweet Potato: medium size
  • Brown Rice: 1 cup
  • Quinoa: 1 cup
  • Wheat Berries: 1 cup
  • Whole Grain Bread: 2 slices
  • Whole Wheat Tortilla
  • Beans: 1/2 can
